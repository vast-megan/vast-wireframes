Destinations
International Space Station −
In 2027, in partnership with NASA, Vast will fly a private astronaut crew to the ISS for a 14-day mission.
Haven-1 +
Haven-1 is the world's first commercial space station. Vast offers customers access to Haven-1 for research, manufacturing, and long-duration crew missions in low-Earth orbit.
Future Haven Stations +
Vast's future Haven stations will provide large-scale commercial platforms in low-Earth orbit, with expanded capacity for long-duration missions, crew habitation, and dedicated customer modules.
International Space Station
Microgravity innovation
[ Science video ]
Our crewed space stations enable microgravity science, research, and manufacturing across biology and biotechnology, physical sciences, technology demonstrations, and human health and performance.
